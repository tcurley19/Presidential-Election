Hillary Diane Rodham Clinton October 26, 1947 Chicago, Illinois Democratic Party Bill Clinton Chelsea Clinton 3 dogs and 1 cat

Hillary Clinton grew up in Chicago, Illinois with her parents and two brothers. She graduated from Yale with a degree in law and later married fellow graduate, Bill Clinton. She served as the First Lady when he became President and later, a U.S. senator. She decided to run for presidency in 2007, but Barack Obama won. During Obama's presidency, Clinton was appointed secretary of state. This year, Clinton is once again running in the presidential election, hoping to be the first female President of the United States.
